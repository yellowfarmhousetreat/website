Order Details & Helpful Notes

Deposits

A 50% deposit is required to lock in your bake date. Remaining balances are due at pickup.

Pickup & Delivery

Pickups are scheduled from our Caldwell farmhouse. Limited local delivery may be available—include the request on your order form.

Shipping

Only Pecan English Toffee can ship nationwide. We'll calculate shipping costs by ZIP code after you submit your order.

⚖️ Cottage Food Operation

Yellow Farmhouse Treats is a cottage food operation that is not subject to Idaho food safety regulations or inspections. Our products are made in a home kitchen that may also process common food allergens.

These products have not been evaluated by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA). These products are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.

Idaho Cottage Food Law permits the sale of certain low-risk food products prepared in home kitchens. For questions about food safety or allergens, please contact us directly.
